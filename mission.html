Our Mission

At EiRA, every purchase creates ripples of positive change — supporting craftsmanship and animal welfare.

Empowering Women

We work directly with women artisans in India, providing fair wages and dignified work that supports their families and communities.

Animal Welfare

A portion of every sale goes to animal welfare initiatives, helping rescue, rehabilitate, and care for animals in need.

Sustainable Craft

We are committed to sustainable practices, using eco-friendly materials and preserving traditional crafting techniques for future generations.

How We Make a Difference

When you purchase an EiRA bag, you are directly contributing to two causes close to our hearts. First, you are supporting the livelihood of talented women artisans in India who pour their skill and love into every stitch.

Second, a portion of our profits goes to animal welfare organizations that rescue, rehabilitate, and provide loving homes for animals. We believe that compassion should extend to all living beings.

Together, we are building a community that values craftsmanship, compassion, and conscious living. Every bag tells a story of hope and change.
